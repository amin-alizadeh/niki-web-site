NIKI Marine Company Ky (NMC)

NIKI Marine Company Ky (NMC) is a family based company established in Finland, in order to serve as the technical advisor and to provide our clients with various services including navigation and publication charts, pre- inspection report, technical and repair support, vessel brokering for new build or used, purchase and sale vessels, consultancy service for investments in Marine Sector and any other related services in maritime industry. In addition, we supply all necessary equipments and all related original and identical machinery spare parts from the best brands available in the market directly on board vessel such as off shore, LPG and Tankers.
