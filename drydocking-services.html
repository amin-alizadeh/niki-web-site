Niki Marine Company

 

 

Providing you with Supply of engaged machinery spare parts for various types of vessels

Mostly all active, operational vessels worldwide require all the advice and technical information for reconditioning or replacing engine parts or other related requirements. Starting with complete and extensive technical specifications, our team provides New, Surplus, Overhauled & Used Ship Machineries and equipment & Spares worldwide to the most competitive prices to satisfy the requirements of each case. Only through one single contact, you will receive supply of various technical spare parts. With our network of expertise located in Europe and Middle East we have extensive knowledge of supplier of spare parts at any strategic ports around the globe.

Through cooperation with reliable and ISO accredited companies, supply and delivery of spare parts is accomplished wherever the requirements of our customers are. In addition, with various brands available for each part in market we are doing our best to supply genuine, identical replacement parts as well as accessory items from the best brands within the marine industry along with some good technical advice to ensure we supply you the correct parts. Our Supply of spare parts include wide range of Main and Auxiliary Engine, Deck machinery, Engine room machinery including parts for pumps, compressors, winches/windlasses, valves, electrical and all other spares or equipment your vessel may require.

Based on our experience and years of partnerships, we have established special connections with many spare part suppliers and traders enabling us to cover most of vessel's potential needs in different ports….With our wealth of knowledge and experience, we can offer you all the advice and technical information you need for reconditioning or replacing engine parts or other related requirements. Requested spare parts can be delivered when a vessel calls a port or for dispatching wherever our clients require.

Contact us for more information on spare parts requirements for marine diesel engines and stationary power diesel generators. We will assist you with the utmost urgency and professionalism.
